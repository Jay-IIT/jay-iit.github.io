About

Engineering AI That Operates at Scale

As Director of AI/ML at FlexTecs, I architect and lead AI strategy for a platform that audits over $1 trillion in annual client transactions and recovers or prevents over $1 billion in payment errors every year. My work spans the full AI lifecycle — from model design and RAG infrastructure to agentic orchestration and production deployment inside FlexTrap, FlexTecs' proprietary SaaS platform.

I'm an active AI security researcher. My deep-dive into Model Context Protocol revealed 11 critical vulnerability classes — including CVE-2025-6514, a maximal-severity remote code execution flaw — shaping how the industry thinks about agentic AI risk in regulated environments.

I contribute to the open-source AI ecosystem through LangChain, LlamaIndex, and Hugging Face, focusing on security hardening, prompt injection mitigation, and enterprise-grade deployment patterns for agentic workflows.
