About

AI Leadership at FlexTecs

As Director of AI/ML at FlexTecs, I lead the AI and machine learning strategy for a company that reviews over $1 trillion in client transactions and recovers or prevents over $1 billion in incorrect payments annually. I'm building intelligent systems that accelerate recovery audits, strengthen payment accuracy, and power FlexTecs' proprietary SaaS platform, FlexTrap.

Beyond my work at FlexTecs, I contribute to open-source AI frameworks including LangChain, LlamaIndex, and Hugging Face, focusing on security hardening and architectural improvements for agentic AI workflows.

My research on Model Context Protocol (MCP) security has identified 11 critical vulnerability classes affecting enterprise AI deployments, including tool poisoning attacks and cross-server context abuse in agentic systems.
